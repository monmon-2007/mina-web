About Me

Hey, They Call Me Mina!

Computer Science is not just a major, but it's something that I enjoy doing. I love designing, building and creating new things that make sense. I founded the Computer Science Club with my Team work right after attending Hack RU 2014. We liked that idea of having a hacker community and designer that have passion for what they do. As any student want to have a job to support himself, I started at First Credit Services INC as an IT support as a way to support myself. However, it made me think deeper about networking, servers and Computer Security. They also introduced me to their Developer team as I worked along with them on our intranet. on the past year I went to different hackathons in many different places such as New Jersey, Maryland, Chicago, Canada and Finally Silicon Valley in San Francisco which was the end of the hacking Season which I have designed "Blocky" along with my awesome team and we won Best Use of Cyanogen Mod SDK, Now, in my junior year, i don't have as much time as I used to before, However my outside learning didn't stop. I'm working on designing my personal website that gets me more involved in java script, CSS and HTML and also I started learning python network programming properties as I think the internet and networking is the next technical revolution.

Anything and everything, and that's why I had choosen Computer Science.
